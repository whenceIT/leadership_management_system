**Core Strategic Anchoring Framework**



The SLMS must be structured around three integrated layers:



**Layer 1: Strategic Destination**

Five-Year $100M Valuation Framework

Defines:

•	Target valuation

•	Revenue expectations

•	Risk thresholds

•	Structural scaling requirements

•	Profitability margins





**Layer 2: Current Operating Reality**

Operational Guidelines Document

Defines:

•	Current branch structure

•	Current staffing norms

•	Current default performance

•	Current loan consultant performance

•	Current revenue averages





**Layer 3: The Gap**

The difference between:

•	Strategic Targets

•	Current Institutional Averages

The SLMS must continuously measure this gap and make it visible.
