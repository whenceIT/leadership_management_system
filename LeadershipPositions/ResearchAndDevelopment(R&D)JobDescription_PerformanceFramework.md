1. JOB PURPOSE
The R&amp;D Coordinator/Manager serves as the architect and guardian of Whence&#39;s
long-term vision and intellectual legacy. This role is not merely about innovation
management but about building the foundation for a 50-100-year institution that will
transform education, research, and community development globally. The R&amp;D
Manager translates Whence&#39;s founding philosophy of using business success to fund
research that creates new solutions for communities into actionable strategy, team
building, and measurable progress toward the establishment of the Whence Institute
of Technology (WIT).
This is a non-revenue-generating but critical legacy-building and future-enabling
role that progresses through Tiers 3, 2, and 1 based on vision realization, research
output, team development, and institutional foundation building.
