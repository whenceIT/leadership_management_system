IT Coordinator / IT Manager Job Description &amp;

Performance Framework

Department: Information Technology
Reports To: Technical Director, Executive Chairperson (once established)
Direct Reports: IT Specialists, Developers, Support Staff, External Vendors
Career Path: IT Coordinator → IT Manager Tier 3 → Tier 2 → Tier 1
Date: 2026

1. JOB PURPOSE
The IT Coordinator/Manager serves as the architect and guardian of Whence&#39;s
technological infrastructure and digital transformation. This role is responsible for
building and maintaining a hybrid IT function that balances operational
stability with game-changing innovation, while reducing outsourcing from 100% to
under 50% or much less. The IT Manager ensures that all technology systems,
especially the ERP, LMS, and Fintech platforms are secure, scalable, integrated, and
aligned with the institution&#39;s $100M valuation pursuit.
This is a non-revenue-generating but critical enablement and risk-mitigation
role that progresses through Tiers 3, 2, and 1 based on system reliability, project
delivery, innovation impact, and measurable contribution to operational efficiency
and digital scalability.

2. KEY RESPONSIBILITIES &amp; STRATEGIC CONTEXT

A. IT Function Architecture &amp; Team Building
 Build In-House IT Capability

 Establish and lead a dual-track IT team: Operations
(70%) and Innovation (30%)
 Reduce outsourcing from 100% to ≤50% within 18 months
 Develop cross-training plans so each team member knows ≥40% of the
other’s domain (LMS, ERP, Withinhere and Eziit)

 Vendor &amp; Outsourcing Management

 Evaluate and manage external IT vendors based on the outsourcing
criteria:
 Non-core or rudimentary tasks
 Specialized skills not available internally
 Short-term capacity needs
 Negotiate contracts, ensure SLA compliance, and maintain vendor
performance dashboards

B. System Implementation &amp; Integration
 ERP System Leadership

 Serve as Technical Lead for the ERP implementation (cloud, APIs,
security, integration)
 Ensure the ERP meets technical specifications: 99.5% uptime, ≤15-minute
sync delay, AES-256/TLS 1.3
 Integrate ERP with: LMS, banking APIs, mobile money (MTN/Airtel),
payroll, and SBUs (Withinhere, Eziit)

 Loans Management System (LMS) Enhancement

 Upgrade LMS to support real-time reconciliation, AI credit scoring, and
fraud detection
 Implement API-based integrations with bank collections, and mobile
payment platforms
 Ensure LMS supports short-term loan lifecycle (1-week to 4-week) and
payroll loan processing

C. Security, Compliance &amp; Risk Management
 Cybersecurity Framework

 Implement and maintain: SSO, MFA, RBAC, encrypted backups, intrusion
detection
 Conduct quarterly security audits and penetration testing
 Ensure compliance with ZICTA, BoZ, and data protection regulations

 Disaster Recovery &amp; Business Continuity

 Design and test DRP with geo-redundant backups and ≤4-hour RTO
 Maintain 7-year data retention for ZRA/audit compliance
 Implement branch-level internet redundancy and failover systems

D. Innovation &amp; Strategic Projects
 Fintech &amp; Digital Product Development

 Lead technical development for Withinhere (e-commerce) and Eziit (zero-
interest product)
 Solve payment gateway reliability and transaction processing challenges

advantage
 AI &amp; Automation Strategy

 Develop AI agents for accounting automation, fraud detection, credit

 Implement RPA for reconciliations, reporting, and compliance monitoring
 Drive efficiency through technology rather than manual expansion

7. PROJECT GOVERNANCE &amp; REPORTING

A. Mandatory Project Reporting (Per IT Guidelines)
For every project/initiative, the following must be documented:
1) Activity Description: Name, expected outcome, example, problem solved
2) Financial Impact: Money saved/lost, revenue increase, likelihood
3) Execution Style: In-house/outsourced/hybrid
4) Cost Estimate &amp; Optimization Options
5) Security &amp; Compliance Considerations
6) Timeframe Estimation: Best case, normal, worst case with component
breakdown

B. Governance Structure
 Weekly: IT-Finance review (ERP integration, reconciliations)
 Monthly: Steering committee (projects, security, compliance)
 Quarterly: Vendor performance review
 Bi-Annually: Technology roadmap review with Executive Chairperson
