1. Job Purpose
The Creative Artworks &amp; Marketing Representative/Manager is a Hybrid Income-
Generating Manager responsible for transitioning the marketing function from a cost
center to a profit center. Initially operating as a Creative Artworks
Representative coordinating marketing materials while managing Loan Consultants
with creative responsibilities, the role can graduate to Marketing Manager upon
demonstrating ability to generate sustainable income, implement aggressive
marketing strategies, and achieve self-funding operations. Seniority and reward are
determined by revenue and net income generated, comparable to branch, district,
and provincial managerial tiers.

3. Key Responsibilities

A. Creative Services Management
 Coordinate creation of all marketing materials (digital, print, promotional)
 Manage brand consistency across all branches
 Oversee production of branded materials (regalia, calendars, office items)
 Implement pre-order system for branch marketing requests

B. Income Generation Activities (Check for details in the income generating activities
document)
1) Branch Monthly Contributions: Maintain and optimize
2) Branding Item Sales: Sell pre-approved branding materials to branches
3) Institutional Supply Bidding: Compete for institutional branding contracts
(company regalia, calendars, etc)
4) Revenue Trading: Participate in new branch/product revenue sharing
5) Client Referral Fees: Vet social media inquiries → branch referrals (K20/fee)
6) Targeted Promotions: Special campaigns for branches (recruitment, client
acquisition)
7) Client Revitalization: Campaigns for lost clients → 10% revenue share
8) Conflict Resolution: Dispute resolution services → K20/successful case
9) New Income Streams: Develop additional revenue-generating services

7. Path to Marketing Manager Promotion

A. Promotion Requirements
1) Financial Sustainability:
 Department covers all operational costs for 6 consecutive months
 Net income ≥ K50,000 monthly for 3 consecutive months
2) Strategic Capability:
 Documented marketing strategy with measurable outcomes
 Proven ability to generate multiple income streams
 Technology implementation improving efficiency
3) Operational Maturity:
 Systematized processes for all services
 Quality control measures in place
 Scalable team structure

B. Benefits Upon Promotion
 Managerial salary and benefits
 Dedicated office space
 Authority to hire dedicated marketing staff
 Marketing budget autonomy
 Participation in senior strategy meetings

9. Performance Management &amp; Consequences

A. Review Cycles
 Monthly: Financial performance and income stream analysis
 Quarterly: Progress toward Marketing Manager promotion
 Bi-Annual: Strategic marketing plan review
 Annual: Tier assessment and compensation review

B. Success Criteria
 Exceeds Expectations: On track for promotion, exceeding income targets
 Meets Expectations: Meeting targets, progressing toward self-funding
 Below Expectations: Missing targets, requiring additional branch funding
 Failure: Consistently unable to generate income → role reassessment

C. Incentive Structure
 Base Allowance: For Creative Artworks Representative level
 Performance Bonus: % of net income generated
 Promotion Bonus: Upon achieving Marketing Manager status
 Innovation Bonus: For new successful income streams
 Referral Commission: Direct percentage of referral fees
