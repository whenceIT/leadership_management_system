Motor Vehicles/Collateral Loans Manager Job
Description for Whence Financial Services

Department: Specialized Lending &amp; Asset-Backed Finance
Reports To: Head of Specialized Products / Technical Director
Direct Reports: Collateral Loan Officers, Asset Recovery Specialists, Valuation
Officers
Location: Headquarters or Regional Hub
Date: 2026

1. Job Purpose
The Motor Vehicles/Collateral Loans Manager is a Revenue &amp; Risk-Balanced
Manager responsible for scaling a profitable portfolio of asset-backed loan products,
primarily motor vehicle collateral loans, while innovating new secured lending products.
The role focuses on achieving strict net profit targets, maintaining 100% collection
compliance, and expanding product reach nationally while operating within a high-
utilization, low-leakage framework. Seniority and rewards are solely determined by
revenue and net income performance relative to branch, district, and provincial
benchmarks.

2. Key Responsibilities

A. Portfolio Management &amp; Profitability
 Manage the motor vehicle collateral loan portfolio (K10,000.00 –
K100,000.00, 1-month tenure).
 Ensure minimum monthly net profit of K40,000 (or K120,000 rolling 3-month).
 Achieve 95%+ utilization of available monthly float.
 Maintain administrative leakage ≤5% of float.

B. Risk &amp; Compliance Oversight
 Ensure 100% repayment compliance across all disbursed loans.
 Maintain performing loan book ratio ≥97%.
 Enforce collateral liquidation within 10 calendar days of default.
 Guarantee 100% adherence to 1-month loan tenure, unless in special
circumstances on approval from supervisor.

C. Growth &amp; Expansion
 Drive 15% month-on-month growth in new client acquisition.
 Onboard 5+ new branches per month to the product.
 Achieve national coverage across all 10 provinces within 6 months.
 Innovate new collateral-backed products beyond motor vehicles.

D. Operational Excellence
 Oversee collateral storage, security, and insurance.
 Manage vehicle valuation and inspection process.
 Ensure timely loan processing and disbursement.
 Maintain accurate asset and loan documentation.

E. Strategic Innovation
 Identify and propose new revenue-generating collateral products.
 Develop variations of secured lending products.
 Explore revenue trading opportunities with other departments.
 Enhance product profitability and risk-adjusted returns.

11. Addendum Compliance Requirements

 Net Profit = Revenue - (All departmental costs + salaries + housing allowances)
 Rolling 3-month calculation permitted for target achievement
 Monthly reporting must clearly show net profit derivation

B. Product Expansion Protocol

2) Risk assessment
3) Profitability projection
4) Implementation plan
