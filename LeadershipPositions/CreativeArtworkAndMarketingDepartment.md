1. Branch Monthly Contributions: Institutional Service Fee
 Description: Mandatory monthly fee paid by each branch for baseline creative and
marketing support services.
 Pricing Model: Tiered based on branch size/disbursement volume (just examples)

 Small Branch (&lt; K300K/month): K300/month
 Medium Branch (K300K–K600K/month): K500/month
 Large Branch (&gt; K600K/month): K800/month

 Services Included:

 Monthly digital marketing content (social media posts, branch-specific
promotions)
 Standard branch branding updates
 Basic promotional material design (up to 5 hours/month)
 Access to marketing asset library

 Optimization Strategy:

 Quarterly service reviews with branches to demonstrate value
 Upsell to premium service tiers with additional features
 Volume discounts for districts with multiple branches

7. Client Revitalization: Lost Client Recovery Program
 Description: Proactive campaign to re-engage clients who have paid off loans but not
returned.
 Process:
1. Database Identification: Monthly report of clients &gt;90 days since last loan
2. Segmentation: Group by reason for departure (if known)
3. Targeted Outreach:

 Personalized emails/SMS
 Special re-engagement offers
 Service improvement communications
4. Revival Tracking: Monitor return and new loan uptake
5. Revenue Share: 10% of first loan amount after return
 Communication Strategy:

 &quot;We Miss You&quot; campaign with special offers
 Service improvement announcements
 New product notifications
 Client appreciation messaging

 Success Metrics:

 15% reactivation rate target
 Average revived loan value: K8,000
 Revenue potential: K12,000/month (150 revivals)

8. Conflict Resolution: Client Retention Services
 Description: Formalized service to resolve client disputes, preventing attrition and
recovering relationships.
 Service Scope:

 Disputed payment amounts
 Service complaints
 Delayed disbursement issues
 Communication breakdowns

 Process:

1) Case Intake: Formal complaint registration
2) Investigation: Fact-finding with branch and client
3) Resolution Proposal: Fair settlement recommendation
4) Implementation: Oversee resolution execution
